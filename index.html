Nimbus 2000

Transport yourself through ASMR to your favourite fictional places

It's always easier to study if you imagine you're at Hogwarts

...or anywhere else, to be exact, except at your muggle school! If you're having trouble with your thoughts being too loud and not letting you concentrate, or your focus may be just out of reach like a snitch at a Quidditch game, don't worry. First of all, you're not alone fellow procrastinator. Second of all, I may have a solution to offer you.

ASMR. Almost at Hogwarts.

These are videos that have a combination of sounds, soft music and slight movements, just so you can stay awake and feel like you're actually at some other place other than your study space. One of my favourite channels is ASMR rooms and if you feel like this may be your spiel, check it out.

Ambiance. Feel the atmosphere.

For you gamers out there, or the non HP folk, here's another great channel called The ASMR Geek. It has a variety of awesome geeky stuff to study or meditate to.

And lastly, binaural beats. All is white noise.

When you just need to tune out everything and let your brain recalibrate, The Brainwave Hub is the place to go.

If you prefer studying in silence but you need that extra push not to open the latest Buzfeed quiz, head over to our gallery for some magical backgrounds that will make you focus like Hermione Granger.
